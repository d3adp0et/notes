====== 5.5 Explain types and purposes of audits and assessments. ======
Created Wednesday 06 August 2025

																				{{./pasted_image_250807.png}}

=== # Attestation ===
	+ Provides an opinion of truth or accuracy of a compnay's security positioning 
	+ An auditor will attest to a company's cybersecurity posture

=== # Internal Audits ===
	+ Compliance: is org compliant
	+ Audit committee: Team that oversees
	+ Self assessments: Own checks, maintain reports

=== # External Audits ===
	+ Regulatory: Audit type and frequency are often based on the regulation
	+ Examination: hands-on research; view records, compile reports, gather additional details
	+ Assessment: current status and future improvements

=== #  Penetration testing ===
	+ Physical pentesting
	+ Offensive
	+ Defensive
	+ Integrated (purple) : do both at the same time
	+ Known Env – White Box – Full disclosure
	+ Partially known Env – Gray Box
	+ Unknown Env – Black Box – Blind test
	+ Reconnaissance
		-+ Passive (OSINT)
		-+ Active (hands-on)
